Privacy Policy

Room Score Tracker · Last updated June 20, 2026

Room Score Tracker ("the app," "we," "us") is a tool that lets a parent or guardian photograph a child's room and bathroom and receive a tidiness score to help manage an allowance. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Account information — your email address and a securely hashed password (we never store your password in plain text). If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive a unique identifier and, where you allow it, your email address.
  • Content you create — the names you give your children, their assigned colors and bathroom-day schedules, the baseline and daily photos you upload, and the scores and allowance settings generated from them.

How we use it

  • To provide the app: storing your children, scores, and photos to your account so they sync across your devices and aren't lost.
  • To grade rooms: the photos you submit for grading are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to produce a tidiness score. They are processed for that request and not used to train models.
  • To send essential account email (such as password-reset links) via our email provider, Resend.

How your data is stored

Your data is stored in a private database hosted on Railway. Each account's data is kept separate, and you can only access your own. We use industry-standard encryption in transit (HTTPS).

Children's information

This app is intended for use by parents and guardians, not by children. The photos and names you add relate to your own children and are entered by you, the adult account holder. We do not knowingly allow children to create accounts, and we do not use this content for advertising or share it with third parties for their own purposes.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share it only with the service providers needed to run the app — Anthropic (image grading), Railway (hosting/database), and Resend (account email) — and only to the extent required to provide their service. We may disclose information if required by law.

Your choices and deletion

  • Delete your account — you can permanently delete your account and all associated data from within the app (Setup → Delete account). This removes your children, scores, photos, and settings from our database and cannot be undone.
  • Access and correction — you can view and edit your data in the app at any time.

Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account, your data is removed from our active database.

Changes

We may update this policy; we'll revise the "Last updated" date above when we do.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Contact the app operator at warrenrayschwartz@gmail.com.
